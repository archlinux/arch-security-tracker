Arch Linux Security

For Arch Linux security is paramount, and a dedicated team of staffers and volunteers helps with triaging, prioritizing, confirming, patching and notifying about vulnerabilities in Arch Linux components as well as packages provided in the official repositories.

Reporting Vulnerablities in Arch Linux Components or Infrastructure

If you have discovered a vulnerability in packages related to core components or in Arch Linux infrastructure don't hesitate to reach out to security@archlinux.org to discuss remediation. You can encrypt your email to the following gpg keys.

Vulnerabilities in Upstream Packages

Our CVE tracker can help you review the vulnerabilities that affect the packages we provide. If you think a CVE that affects a package is not listed in there, do not hesitate to reach out to us in irc on #archlinux-security in freenode.
